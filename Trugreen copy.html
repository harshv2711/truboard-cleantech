AI-driven plant monitoring and diagnostics solution designed exclusively for the solar energy sector. SolarVision.AI

start a conversation
  • Seamlessly incorporates thermal and normal drone images, supporting images captured at optimal flying heights and camera angles.
  • Automated pre-processing, model-based fault detection, confidence scoring, and fault labeling—all within minutes.
  • Identifies soiling quality, vegetation cover, multi-cell hotspots, diode and string failures, module breakage, string shorting, cracked modules, module short circuits, and single cell hotspots.

  • Processes approximately 3000 data tables from a single drone flight, equivalent to analyzing nearly 40 MW of solar capacity, ensuring scalable diagnostics for large installations.
